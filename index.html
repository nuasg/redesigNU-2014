Calling all designers, developers, and innovators!

Join us for the first ever Northwestern Hackathon

  • Learn

    Be the one of the first to use never before released data, such as the Caesar Class Search API.

  • Network

    Network and socialize with like-minded people, as well as expand your professional network with tech representatives and Northwestern administators.

  • Create

    Bring original ideas into the student experience.

  • Win

    Showcase your skill set in front of a distinguished panel of judges, the Northwestern community, and win great prizes

Who can come?

You! Anyone currently enrolled in Northwestern, graduate and undergraduate, is able to register. We recommend, but is not required, that teams be made up of program managers, graphic designers, and programmers.

Can I register as a team?

Absolutely! We encourage people to register in teams of 1-6. Just include your team members' names on the registration page. And don't forget that each team member much submit a separate registration!

I don't have a team, what about me?

No worries - there will be time for groups of 1-6 to form teams throughout registration.

I don't code, what about me?

You've found the perfect place and time to learn! We'll have mentors during the event to help participants.

Don't want to use any of the data at the event?

That's great- all ideas that will improve the student experience, in any way, are welcomed!
